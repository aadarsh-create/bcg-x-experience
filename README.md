# BCG X — Data Science & Advanced Analytics Job Simulation

Completed the **BCG X Data Science & Advanced Analytics Job Simulation** on Forage — a hands-on simulation of real consulting work done by BCG's data science teams.

---

## Objective

PowerCo, a major energy utility, is experiencing significant SME customer churn in a liberalized energy market. The initial business hypothesis was that price sensitivity is the primary driver of churn.
Can we predict which SME customers of PowerCo are likely to churn, and is a targeted 20% discount the most cost-effective retention strategy?

---

## Key Finding

Historical pricing and usage data reveals that while price changes correlate with churn, **tenure and contract timing are equally strong predictors** — price alone is insufficient to explain churn behavior.

---

## Outcome

A **Random Forest model (~85% accuracy)** was built to identify the top 10% of high-churn-risk customers. Targeting this segment with a 20% discount was recommended as the most cost-effective retention strategy, potentially saving PowerCo significant revenue.
